Ionosat – Micro

is the Ukrainian satellite mission, realized under the National purpose scientific and technical space program of Ukraine

Project task is the study of dynamic processes in the ionosphere, through synchronized space and ground-based measurements to explore the relationship between ionospheric disturbances and processes on the Sun, in the magnetosphere, atmosphere and Earth interior.

Patricular project tasks

Formation of reliable and representative data sets of the ionospheric perturbations along the orbital track depending on helio- and geophysical conditions (solar and magnetic activity, latitude, UT, etc.);

Development and testing of the scientific equipment to record electro- and gas-dynamical parameters of the ionosphere in flight conditions;

Working of techniques for the ionosphere monitoring by space and ground-based facilities;

Verification of technical solutions of the developed the satellite platform;

Database and web-resource creation of mission results. The inclusion of collected data into the global geoinformatic systems;

Utilization of obtained scientific results in the educational process. Popularization of space research.

What registration

global dynamic structure and physical parameters of the neutral atmosphere (exosphere) and ionospheric plasma;

detailed spatial structure and physical parameters of the ionospheric current systems and geomagnetic field;

physical parameters of the ULF-ELF-VLF electromagnetic wave structures.

Methodological principles

realization aboard one satellite complete diagnostics of ionosphere parameters by contact methods in a wide range of temporal and spatial scales;

priority of monitoring measurements to create a representative data sample of ionospheric observations;

developing of database of disturbed ionosphere parameters in the interests of research in the field of fundamental ionosphere physics, space weather, terragenic and anthropogenic effects in the near Earth space.

Ionosat-Micro scientific instrumentation

General characteristics of «Ionosat-Micro» scientific instrumentation set

Weight < 20 kg
Power consumption < 51 W
Data downstream up to 6 GB per day

On board data handling system (OBDH)

Input data stream up to 100 Mbit/s. Output data stream up to 64 Mbit/s. Storage amount 512 GB
PI: Adolf Lukeniuk, luk@isr.lviv.ua

Magneto-wave complex (MWC)

Including flux-gate magnetometer FGM, 3 wave probes WP, electric probe ЕР

Measured parameters: 3 components of the magnetic field (0—18500 Hz), 3 components of the electric field (1—18500 Hz), 3 components of the electric current density in plasma (1—18500 Hz)

PI: Sergey Belyayev, belyayev@isr.lviv.ua

Radio-frequency analyzer RFA

Measured parameters: 3-component electric field spectra (20 kHz — 15 MHz)

PI: Hanna Rothkaehl, hrot@cbk.waw.pl

Particles density analyzer (PDA)

Including neutral gas sensor DN and electronic plasma component sensor DE

Measured parameters: Concentration and temperature of neutral gas(10 5 —10 11 cm -3 , 600—2000 К); concentration and temperature of thermal electrons (10 3 —10 8 cm -3 , 0,1—1 eV).

PI: Valentyn Shuvalov, shuv@vash.dp.ua

Ion driftmeter ID-2

Including IDM sensor (driftmeter) and RPA sensor (ion trap)

Measured parameters: Ion concentration (10 2 —10 7 cm -3 ), temperature (500—7000 К), ion drift velocity (10—5000 m/s) and spectrum of ion concentration inhomogeneities (10—2000 Hz).

PI: Ludmil Bankov, ludmil.bankov@gmail.com

Database

Measurement data are stored at the Data processing, storage and distribution center PROMIS. The project participants have access to all data levels. Six months after the date of observation the second-level data are placed in the public domain.

DATA STRUCTURE
Magneto-wave complex MWC 3 components of DC magnetic field 0–1 Hz in the spacecraft reference system
3 components of AC magnetic field 1 Hz – F max
3 components of AC electric field 1 Hz – F max
3 components of AC electric current density 1 Hz – F max
The maximal frequencies F max HF measurements 18,5 kHz
Quick monitoring 11,9 kHz
Slow monitoring 375 Hz
Radio-frequency analyzer RFA 3-component spectra of electric field 20 kHz – 15 MHz (sampling 1 Hz)
Neutral gas sensor DN Concentration and temperature of neutral gas Sampling 10 Hz
Electronic plasma component sensor DE Concentration and temperature of thermal electrons Sampling 10 Hz
Ion driftmeter ID-2 Ion concentration, temperature and ion drift velocity, spectrum of ion inhomogeneities Sampling 10 Hz
